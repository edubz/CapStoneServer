In & Out Of Euclidean Closeness, We Disregard Distance To Take Digital Pills & Dive Into The Algorithm Life

The embedded radio player is a live stream of an audio system made in Max 8, which involves using web servers to collect user submitted audio files from this website.

The audio files are then processed through a variety of effects with parameters that can be controlled wirelessly via a limited amount of collectible sensor-filled devices.

This website, the Max patch, and all microcontroller devices designed, handcrafted, and maintained by PCC Creative Coding Capstone students. Source code here
